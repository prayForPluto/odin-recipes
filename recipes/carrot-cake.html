Carrot Cake

Description

This delicious award winning carrot cake is sure to lure rabbits out of their holes and guaranteed to please even the most picky in-law during the holidays.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups of oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 tsp of baking soda
  • 2 tsp of baking powder
  • 2 tsp of cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  • 3 cups of grated carrots, packed
  • 3/4 cups of walnuts

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 360 F with a rack in the middle position. Liberally grease a pan
  2. Beat the sugar and oil together. Beat in eggs one at a time
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt
  4. Add the flour mixture to the sugar mixture and mix with a wooden spoon
  5. Fold in the carrots and walnuts
  6. Pour mixture into greased pan and bake for 45 to 60 min
